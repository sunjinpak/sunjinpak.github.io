# Project 6: Leader Workaholism & Food Safety

**Title**: The Cost of the Grind: Assessing the Impact of Leader Workaholism on Food Safety Compliance and Employee Retention

**Team**: Eric

**Competition**: Grimm Family Center Student Research Symposium

---

## Overview

This study examines how leader workaholism affects food safety compliance and employee retention in the agriculture and food processing industry. Drawing on Conservation of Resources (COR) theory, the project investigates whether supervisors who exhibit workaholic behaviors create conditions that deplete employee resources, leading to emotional exhaustion, reduced food safety compliance, and higher turnover intentions. The study uses an online survey targeting 100-150 employees in the agriculture and food processing sector.

## 2. Survey

**Survey Link**: [https://food-safety-survey.netlify.app](https://food-safety-survey.netlify.app)

### Target Participants

- Employees aged 18+ in the agriculture or food processing industry
- Target sample size: 100-150 participants
- Recruitment: LinkedIn and snowball sampling

### Survey Measures

| Measure | Items | Source |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Leader Workaholism | 10 | Dutch Work Addiction Scale (Schaufeli et al., 2009) |
| Food Safety Compliance | 6 | Safety Compliance Scale (Neal & Griffin, 2006) |
| Turnover Intention | 3 | Colarelli (1984) |
| After-Hours Work Contact | 5 | Derks et al. (2015) |
| Emotional Exhaustion | 5 | Maslach Burnout Inventory |
| Job Satisfaction | 3 | Michigan Organizational Assessment Questionnaire |
| Demographics | 7 | Age, gender, tenure, job type, industry sector, shift, education |

---

## 3. Research Questions

- **H1**: Leader workaholism is negatively related to employee food safety compliance.
- **H2**: Leader workaholism is positively related to employee turnover intention.
- **H3**: After-hours work contact is positively related to emotional exhaustion.
- **H4**: Emotional exhaustion mediates the relationship between leader workaholism and turnover intention.
- **H5**: Job satisfaction mediates the relationship between leader workaholism and turnover intention.
